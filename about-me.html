About Me

Hello, there. My name, as given to me at my ascension through the crust of earth is, Mikaere Henare, which is Maori or "Kiwi" for Michael Henry which trust me I'm fully aware is the most simple white male name with the most found english second name to follow it, so dont let the exotic sounding nature of my name fool you there.
